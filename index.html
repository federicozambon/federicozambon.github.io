Droid's Chronicles

3D Twin stick shooter in development with Unity3D.
Featuring 6 different enemies with unique AIs, 4 maps with unique traps and mechanics.
Core mechanics are an endless wave system, a score/combo system and a set of power up that the player can buy with the game credits.
Role: Lead programmer. Mechanics implemented: AIs with state machines, player aim and shoot system, power ups, score system.

WaveOn()

Two days project done during the Global Game Jam 2017 in Politecnico di Milano with other 4 programmers and 5 artists.
It consists in a two players local coop game that is based on communication. It features two totally different points of view (First person and Top down) that are rendered on two different screens.
